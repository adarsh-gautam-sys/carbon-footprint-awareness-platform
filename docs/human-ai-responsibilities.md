# Human vs AI Responsibilities

## Human Responsibilities

- Define the real-world problem and target users.
- Decide the data categories users can reasonably provide.
- Review carbon factors and update them as better local data becomes available.
- Configure Google Cloud project, billing, IAM, Secret Manager, and deployment access.
- Validate recommendations before public launch or classroom/community use.

## AI Responsibilities

- Interpret user profile and activity data.
- Convert carbon results into concise, personalized guidance.
- Prioritize actions based on likely impact and effort.
- Keep advice non-judgmental and practical.
- Fall back gracefully when Gemini is not configured.

## Shared Responsibilities

- Keep user data minimal and purposeful.
- Avoid overstating precision in carbon estimates.
- Improve prompts and calculations based on testing feedback.
- Monitor deployment logs and user experience after release.
