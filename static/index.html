    How You Compare

    What is CO₂e?

    CO₂ equivalent (CO₂e) combines all greenhouse gases — carbon dioxide, methane, nitrous oxide — into a single comparable unit based on their warming effect.

    Why does it matter?

    The average global citizen needs to reach under 2 tonnes CO₂e/year by 2050 to limit warming to 1.5 °C. Most people in developed countries are 3–10× over that target.

    Methodology

    Estimates use published IPCC and EPA emission factors. Calculations are transparent and deterministic. Gemini AI personalises the phrasing of insights — it does not change the numbers.
